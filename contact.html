Frequently Asked Questions Često postavljana pitanja

What is your typical project timeline? Koliko obično traje projekat?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple projects can take 2-4 weeks, while complex enterprise solutions may take 3-6 months or more. Vreme trajanja projekta zavisi od složenosti i obima. Jednostavni projekti mogu trajati 2-4 nedelje, dok složena poslovna rešenja mogu trajati 3-6 meseci ili više.

Do you provide ongoing support and maintenance? Da li pružate kontinuiranu podršku i održavanje?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages to ensure your software continues to perform optimally after deployment. Da, nudimo sveobuhvatne pakete podrške i održavanja kako bi vaš softver nastavio da radi optimalno nakon implementacije.

What technologies do you work with? Sa kojim tehnologijama radite?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including React, Angular, Node.js, Python, Java, .NET, AWS, Azure, and many more. Radimo sa širokim spektrom modernih tehnologija uključujući React, Angular, Node.js, Python, Java, .NET, AWS, Azure i mnoge druge.
